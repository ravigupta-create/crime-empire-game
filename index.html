How to Play

Walk around The Row and approach NPCs. Press E to talk, threaten, bribe, deal, recruit, or shakedown. Build rep to unlock new districts, crew slots, and abilities. Manage your heat — cops get aggressive when it rises. Earn dirty money, launder it through front businesses, and expand your territory block by block.
100% free. Zero dependencies. Runs entirely in your browser.
